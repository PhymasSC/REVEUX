What is REVEUX?

REVEUX, an online beauty store founded in 2021. In the year 2020, the global has been battered by covid-19 pandemic. This pandemic changed the lifestyle of people all around the world. The online business started becomes the trend and everyone can buy anything through an online store and the things will be delivered to the buyer. Therefore, we get an business idea to open an online beauty store. REVEUX has no physical store, all of the deals and transactions are done throught the REVEUX officail website. REVEUX offers various type of cosmetic products such as skincare products, haircare products, body care products, make-up products, and fragrance.
